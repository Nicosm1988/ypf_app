# Style Guide

## Voice

Use formal Argentine Spanish with a practical academic tone. The portal should sound like a senior BI/product lead explaining work to management and to the daily team at the same time.

## Structure

- Start with the conclusion.
- Continue with three non-overlapping reasons.
- Close with evidence, action, or control.
- Use titles as assertions: `La salida a producciĂ³n empieza antes del deploy.`
- Avoid labels that do not decide anything: `IntroducciĂ³n`, `Conceptos`, `Resumen`.

## Fluency

Use connectors naturally: `por eso`, `en ese sentido`, `sin embargo`, `a partir de ahi`, `dicho de otro modo`, `en consecuencia`, `al mismo tiempo`.

Avoid robotic phrases: `es importante destacar`, `cabe mencionar`, `en el mundo actual`, `potenciar capacidades`, `soluciĂ³n robusta` unless they are technically precise.

## Hard Rules

- Do not mention hidden writing methods or consulting frameworks in visible copy.
- Do not use forced HTML line breaks.
- Do not add a concept unless it changes a decision, evidence, risk, or operation.
